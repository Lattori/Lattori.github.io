Race and Intersectionality

How Race and Intersectionality can affect an individual, June 2, 2024

Image Source

Hey Everyone, Welcome to my Blog! Let's explore the concept of intersectionality and why it's important. For the last few centuries, Men have dominated most societies creating patriarchal societies throughout the world. While this generally benefits a percentage of men, others (specifically marginalized groups) do not receive the same benefits as others. The most common example of this would be LGBTQIA+ men, who are often discriminated against because of their affiliation with queer and transgender communities. Additionally, they are also neglected by society as a whole. An example would be the act of gay marriage, as until a decade or two ago was not legalized and was condemned . Can you think of different ways in which intersectionality affects others and perhaps yourself? Thanks for reading, stay tuned for the next blog post!
